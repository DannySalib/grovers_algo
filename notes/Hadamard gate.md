Square root NOT gate

$$H=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix}1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1\end{pmatrix}$$
$\ket{0}$ ---H--- $\frac{\ket{0}+\ket{1}}{2}$
$\ket{1}$ ---H--- $\frac{\ket{0}-\ket{1}}{2}$

***Note***
$H^2\not=$[[Pauli X Gate (NOT gate)]]
$H^2=I$ 
Therefore...
***Applying H gate twice does nothing to the Qbit***
