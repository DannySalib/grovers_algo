**The key ingredient in quantum teleportation and super-dense coding**

$$\frac{\ket{00}+\ket{11}}{\sqrt{2}}$$
### ***Properties***
When measuring the Qbit
- the probability of the first Qbit being $0$ is $\frac{1}{2}$
	- making the post-measurement state $\ket{\varphi'}=\ket{00}$
- and the probability of $1$ being $\frac{1}{2}$
	- $\ket{\varphi'}=\ket{11}$
	
Therefore...
***The measurement of the second Qbit is always the same as the measurement of the first Qbit***
