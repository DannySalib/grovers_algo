### **Qbit Pair***
$\ket{00},\ket{01},\ket{10},\ket{11}$
### **Superposition of a Qbit Pair***
$\ket{\psi}=\alpha_{00}+\alpha_{01}\ket{01}+\alpha_{10}\ket{10}+\alpha_{11}\ket{11}$
### ***Probability***
$P(X)=\lvert{\alpha_{x}}\lvert^{2} \quad\forall{X}\in{00, 01, 10, 11}$
- given $\sum_{x\in\{0,1\}^2}\lvert{\alpha_x}\lvert^2=1$
- where $\{0,1\}^2$ means: set of strings of length 2 whose letters are either 0 or 1
### **System***
You can measure a subset of Qbits within a system, measure it's probability, and predict the probability of the other Qbit
- Measuring the first Qbit is $0$ with the probability $|{\alpha_{00}}^{2}|+|{\alpha_{01}}^2|$
Therefore...
$$\ket{\psi'}=\frac{\alpha_{00}\ket{00}+\alpha_{01}\ket{01}}{\sqrt{|\alpha_{00}|^2+|\alpha_{01}|^2}}$$
